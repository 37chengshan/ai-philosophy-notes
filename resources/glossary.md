## Part I: 死亡哲学核心术语

### A. 本体论术语

#### **Death (死亡)**

**定义**: The termination of life or existence.

**来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy; Yale "Philosophy: Death" course

**哲学理解**:
- **Physicalism**: 死亡=身体毁灭=存在终止
- **Dualism**: 死亡=身体毁灭，灵魂可能幸存
- **Existentialism**: 死亡作为存在可能性（Heidegger）
- **Eastern philosophy**: 死亡作为流转/过渡（轮回、气化）

**相关术语**: Mortality, Extinction, Being-toward-death

---

#### **Mortality (必死性)**

**定义**: The condition of being subject to death; human finitude as an existential condition.

**来源**: Heidegger, *Being and Time*; Existential philosophy

**哲学理解**:
- 不是简单的"会死"事实
- 而是存在的本质条件（有限性）
- 必死性构成存在意义的理解

**相关术语**: Finitude, Being-toward-death, Death anxiety

---

#### **Finitude (有限性)**

**定义**: The inherent limitation or boundedness of human existence.

**来源**: Heidegger, *Being and Time*; Existential philosophy

**哲学理解**:
- 人的存在是有限的（时间、能力、可能性）
- 有限性是存在的前提条件
- 无限追求是非本己的逃避

**相关术语**: Mortality, Infinity, Immortality debate

---

#### **Extinction (消亡)**

**定义**: Complete annihilation or cessation of existence.

**来源**: Yale "Philosophy: Death" course; Physicalist perspective

**哲学理解**:
- 物理主义观点：死亡=完全消亡
- 无死后存在可能性
- 与"生存"(Survival)概念对立

**相关术语**: Annihilation, Survival, Physicalism

---

#### **Survival (生存/幸存)**

**定义**: Continuation of existence after death.

**来源**: Yale "Philosophy: Death" course; Dualist perspective

**哲学理解**:
- 二元论观点：灵魂可能幸存
- 形式：灵魂幸存、复活、转世、幽灵存在
- 同一性问题是核心问题

**相关术语**: Afterlife, Reincarnation, Resurrection, Dualism

---

#### **Afterlife (死后世界)**

**定义**: Existence or state after physical death.

**来源**: Religious traditions; Dualist philosophy

**哲学理解**:
- 宗教传统：天堂、地狱、涅槃等
- 二元论：灵魂在死后世界的存在
- 物理主义通常否认死后世界

**相关术语**: Survival, Heaven, Hell, Nirvana

---

#### **Immortality (不朽/永生)**

**定义**: Eternal existence or endless life.

**来源**: Williams "The Makropulos Case"; Philosophical debate

**哲学理解**:
- **Religious**: 通过信仰获得永生
- **Philosophical**: 永生是否值得追求？（Williams vs Nagel）
- **Confucian**: 立德不朽（道德成就超越死亡）

**相关术语**: Eternal life, Resurrection, Immortality debate

---

### B. 存在主义术语

#### **Being-toward-death (Sein-zum-Tode, 向死而生)**

**定义**: Death as a possibility that shapes Dasein's way of existing.

**来源**: Heidegger, *Being and Time*, §50

**哲学理解**:
- 死亡不是简单的终结，而是存在的一种可能性
- 死亡作为"不可能的可能性"
- 死亡影响此在的存在方式

**误读警示**:
- ✗ 简化为"接受死亡必然性"
- ✗ 简化为"珍惜时间"
- ✓ 正确：死亡作为存在可能性影响存在方式

**相关术语**: Authenticity, Inauthenticity, Finitude

---

#### **Authenticity (Eigentlichkeit, 本己性/真诚性)**

**定义**: Being true to one's own self; facing death and existence in a genuine, personal way.

**来源**: Heidegger, *Being and Time*

**哲学理解**:
- 真诚面对死亡可能性
- 将死亡纳入存在理解
- 不逃避，不沉沦于"常人"

**相关术语**: Inauthenticity, Being-toward-death, das Man

---

#### **Inauthenticity (Uneigentlichkeit, 非本己性/不真诚)**

**定义**: Evasion of one's own existence; falling into impersonal, everyday existence.

**来源**: Heidegger, *Being and Time*

**哲学理解**:
- 逃避死亡（沉沦于日常）
- 以"常人"(das Man)的方式存在
- 通过"人人都会死"淡化个人死亡

**相关术语**: Authenticity, das Man, Falling

---

#### **das Man (常人/他们)**

**定义**: The impersonal "they" or "one" that represents everyday, conformist existence.

**来源**: Heidegger, *Being and Time*

**哲学理解**:
- "常人"代表非本己的逃避方式
- "人人都会死"是常人的逃避表达
- 沉沦于常人是非本己性

**相关术语**: Inauthenticity, Falling, Publicness

---

### C. Yale课程术语

#### **Dualism (二元论)**

**定义**: View that humans consist of two parts: body and soul.

**来源**: Yale "Philosophy: Death" course; Traditional philosophy

**哲学理解**:
- 人=身体+灵魂
- 死亡=身体毁灭，灵魂可能幸存
- 支持死后生存的可能性

**相关术语**: Physicalism, Soul, Survival

---

#### **Physicalism (物理主义)**

**定义**: View that humans are purely physical bodies without souls.

**来源**: Yale "Philosophy: Death" course; Contemporary philosophy

**哲学理解**:
- 人仅是物理身体
- 死亡=身体毁灭=终结
- 通常导致死亡=消亡，但不必然

**相关术语**: Dualism, Materialism, Annihilation

---

#### **Harm Thesis (伤害论题)**

**定义**: Claim that death can harm the person who dies.

**来源**: Epicurus; Nagel; Yale "Philosophy: Death" course

**哲学理解**:
- **Epicurus反对**: 死亡时我们不存在，无法被伤害
- **Deprivation支持**: 死亡剥夺未来好生活，因此有害

**相关术语**: Deprivation account, Timing puzzle, Epicurus

---

#### **Deprivation Account (剥夺论证)**

**定义**: Death harms by depriving the person of future good life.

**来源**: Nagel; Feldman; Yale "Philosophy: Death" course

**哲学理解**:
- 死亡剥夺未来可能的好生活
- 被剥夺是伤害的一种形式
- 支持伤害论题

**相关术语**: Harm thesis, Timing puzzle, Epicurus

---

#### **Timing Puzzle (时机难题)**

**定义**: Problem of when death harms the person if harm thesis is accepted.

**来源**: Yale "Philosophy: Death" course; Death philosophy literature

**哲学理解**:
- 如果死亡有害，何时有害？
- 死前？死亡时？死后？
- 死后不存在，如何被伤害？

**解决方案**:
- **Priorism**: 死前被伤害
- **Concurrentism**: 死亡时被伤害
- **Subsequentism**: 死后被伤害（悖论）
- **Indefinitism**: 不特定时间被伤害

**相关术语**: Harm thesis, Deprivation account

---

### D. 东方哲学术语

#### **Impermanence (Anicca, 无常)**

**定义**: The doctrine that all phenomena are transient and constantly changing.

**来源**: Buddhist philosophy; Buddhist sutras

**哲学理解**:
- 一切现象暂时、生灭流转
- 无常是自然法则，而非虚无
- 认识无常促进觉醒和解脱

**误读警示**:
- ✗ 虚无主义误读："无常=什么都没意义"
- ✗ 悲观主义误读："无常=绝望"
- ✓ 正确：无常是自然法则，意义在于觉醒

**相关术语**: Samsara, Nirvana, Dukkha

---

#### **Samsara (轮回)**

**定义**: The cycle of birth, death, and rebirth.

**来源**: Buddhist philosophy; Hindu philosophy

**哲学理解**:
- 生死循环，不断流转
- 轮回是因缘复杂的流转
- 可通过涅槃超越

**相关术语**: Nirvana, Impermanence, Rebirth

---

#### **Nirvana (涅槃)**

**定义**: The state of perfect peace and liberation from samsara.

**来源**: Buddhist philosophy; Buddhist sutras

**哲学理解**:
- 超越轮回的寂静境界
- 涅槃不是死亡，而是超越
- 死亡在涅槃视角下失去恐惧意义

**相关术语**: Samsara, Liberation, Enlightenment

---

#### **Anatta (无我)**

**定义**: The doctrine that there is no permanent, unchanging self.

**来源**: Buddhist philosophy; Buddhist sutras

**哲学理解**:
- 无固定不变的自我
- 因此无"我的死亡"
- 死亡是因缘流转，非自我终结

**相关术语**: Impermanence, Self, Identity

---

#### **慎终追远 (Shen zhong zhui yuan)**

**定义**: Careful attention to funeral rites and remembrance of distant ancestors.

**来源**: Confucian philosophy; *Analects*

**哲学理解**:
- 慎终：慎重对待丧终
- 追远：追念远祖
- 礼仪实践促进道德厚植

**相关术语**: Filial piety, Ancestor veneration, Funeral rites

---

#### **丧礼 (Sang li)**

**定义**: Funeral rites as ethical practice in Confucian tradition.

**来源**: Confucian philosophy; *Book of Rites*

**哲学理解**:
- 死亡礼仪作为伦理实践
- 礼仪规范情感表达
- 礼仪维系伦理关系

**相关术语**: 祭礼, 慎终追远, Filial piety

---

#### **祭礼 (Ji li)**

**定义**: Sacrificial rites that maintain relationship with the deceased.

**来源**: Confucian philosophy; *Book of Rites*

**哲学理解**:
- 祭祀礼仪维系与逝者关系
- 死亡不是关系的终结
- 通过祭祀延续伦理关系

**相关术语**: 丧礼, Ancestor veneration, 祖先崇拜

---

#### **立德不朽 (Li de bu xiu)**

**定义**: Achieving moral excellence that transcends death.

**来源**: Confucian philosophy; Three immortalities (立德、立功、立言)

**哲学理解**:
- 通过道德成就超越死亡
- 死亡不是意义的终结
- 不朽通过道德、功业、言说实现

**相关术语**: Immortality, Three immortalities, Moral achievement

---

#### **生死一如 (Sheng si yi ru)**

**定义**: Life and death as essentially the same, continuous transformation.

**来源**: Daoist philosophy; *Zhuangzi*

**哲学理解**:
- 生与死本质相同
- 生死是气的聚散流转
- 生死不是对立，而是转化

**相关术语**: 气化, 道法自然, Natural acceptance

---

#### **道法自然 (Dao fa zi ran)**

**定义**: Following the natural way; accepting death as natural process.

**来源**: Daoist philosophy; *Dao De Jing*

**哲学理解**:
- 死亡作为自然过程
- 顺应自然而非对抗
- 接受生死流转

**相关术语**: 生死一如, Naturalness, Non-attachment

---

#### **气化 (Qi hua)**

**定义**: Death as the dispersal and gathering of qi (energy/vital force).

**来源**: Daoist philosophy; *Zhuangzi*

**哲学理解**:
- 死亡是气的聚散
- 气化是自然过程
- 生死是气的不同状态

**相关术语**: 生死一如, Qi, Natural transformation

---

### E. 宗教传统术语

#### **Resurrection (复活)**

**定义**: Rising from death to new life, particularly in Christian tradition.

**来源**: Christian tradition; *New Testament*

**哲学理解**:
- 死后的复活盼望
- 通过信仰获得永生
- 死亡作为过渡而非终结

**相关术语**: Eternal life, Salvation, Christian afterlife

---

#### **Eternal life (永生)**

**定义**: Endless existence after death through faith.

**来源**: Christian tradition; *New Testament*

**哲学理解**:
- 通过信仰获得永生
- 死亡不是终结
- 永生作为宗教承诺

**相关术语**: Resurrection, Salvation, Immortality

---

#### **Akhirah (后世)**

**定义**: The afterlife in Islamic tradition.

**来源**: Islamic tradition; Quran

**哲学理解**:
- 后世生命
- 死后审判
- 天堂(Jannah)或地狱

**相关术语**: Barzakh, Judgment Day, Islamic afterlife

---

#### **Barzakh (中间状态)**

**定义**: Intermediate state between death and resurrection in Islamic tradition.

**来源**: Islamic tradition; Quran

**哲学理解**:
- 死亡与复活间的中间状态
- 灵魂等待审判
- 伊斯兰特有概念

**相关术语**: Akhirah, Judgment Day, Islamic eschatology

### C. 哲学误读术语

#### **Nihilistic misinterpretation (虚无主义误读)**

**定义**: Mistakenly interpreting Eastern philosophy as nihilism.

**来源**: Bias detection framework

**示例**: 将佛教"无常"误读为"什么都没意义"

**相关术语**: Impermanence, Buddhism, Misinterpretation type

---

#### **Simplification bias (简化偏见)**

**定义**: Oversimplifying complex philosophical concepts.

**来源**: Bias detection framework

**示例**: 将儒家死亡简化为"孝道"

**相关术语**: Cultural simplification, Conceptual simplification

---

#### **Western-centric bias (西方中心主义偏见)**

**定义**: Preference for Western philosophical frameworks as default.

**来源**: Bias detection framework

**示例**: 存在主义框架作为默认死亡理解

**相关术语**: Cultural bias, Framework default bias

---

#### **Framework confusion (框架混淆)**

**定义**: Mixing different philosophical frameworks incorrectly.

**来源**: Bias detection framework

**示例**: 用西方框架解释东方死亡概念

**相关术语**: Framework mixing, Tradition misinterpretation
